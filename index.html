About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the area of Geographic Information Systems at NIIT University. My research focuses on Geospatial Intelligence, integrating geospatial technology with AI. I have extensive experience in Microwave Remote Sensing and have published over 15 journal articles indexed in Web of Science and Scopus.

Research Interests

  • Geospatial Intelligence
  • Microwave Remote Sensing
  • GeoAI for Environmental Monitoring
  • Cloud-based Geospatial Data Processing
  • Machine Learning in Remote Sensing

Selected Publications

  • Flood Dynamics in Kaziranga National Park, CATENA Journal, 2024
  • GeoAI-Powered Decision Support System for Biodiversity Monitoring, Remote Sensing Journal, 2023
