Professional Profile

Dr. Thota Sivasankar is an Assistant Professor – Selection Grade at the School of Computer Science, UPES Dehradun. His academic and research work focuses on remote sensing, GIS, microwave SAR, machine learning, and geospatial intelligence for environmental and agricultural applications.

His work integrates satellite remote sensing, geospatial modelling, and AI-driven methods for crop monitoring, soil moisture retrieval, flood susceptibility, forest and biodiversity applications, landslide susceptibility mapping, and disaster risk reduction.
