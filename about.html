Biography

Dr. Thota Sivasankar is an academic and researcher working at the intersection of remote sensing, GIS, microwave SAR, machine learning, and geospatial intelligence. He currently serves as Assistant Professor – Selection Grade at the School of Computer Science, UPES Dehradun.

His research emphasizes satellite-based environmental monitoring, geospatial modelling, SAR image analysis, agricultural applications, disaster risk assessment, and AI-enabled geospatial decision-support systems.

Education

  • Ph.D. in Earth Sciences, IIT (ISM) Dhanbad. Thesis: Vegetation Biophysical Parameters Retrieval using RISAT-1 Hybrid Polarimetric SAR.
  • M.Tech. in Remote Sensing, Andhra University.
  • B.Tech. in Electronics and Communication Engineering.

Professional Experience

  • Assistant Professor – Selection Grade, School of Computer Science, UPES Dehradun.
  • Assistant Professor, GIS Area, NIIT University, Neemrana.
  • Research Scientist, NESAC, North Eastern Space Applications Centre.
  • Research Scholar, Indian Institute of Remote Sensing, ISRO.
